I am Mechanical Engineer, my core interest in the Mechanical Design, Manufacturing and Energy management. Design aesthestic gives creative visualization and satisfaction. I designed and manufactured many prototypes for my research while building cryogenic set up. I had good Software skills in MATLAB, Solidworks, Ansys and Abaquas. I worked Under Dr. Kenneth Park at Northwestern University.I have Bachelor degree and Master of Science degree in Mechanical Engineering.
